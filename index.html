Insight 2 — Posting frequency drives calls

March is a universal seasonal peak (admission season). But Yelahanka shows the clearest posting effect: grew while being posted to, dropped once posting stopped — matching direct same-day-results observation.

Malleshwaram was suspended ~8 days in June — its 291 calls that month still outperformed Koramangala and Jayanagar's full-month totals.

Insight 3 — Bulk-publishing is a suspension risk

Early May: first post published to Yelahanka only. Later May: access extended to all 4 profiles. Second round: a bulk post published across all 4 locations simultaneously. June: Malleshwaram suspended ~8 days, then reinstated.

Causation isn't proven, but the timing matches a known risk pattern. Recommendation: post to each branch individually going forward.
